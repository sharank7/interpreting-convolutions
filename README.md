# Interpreting Convolutions

## Overview
An AI Club Deputy Coordinator Mini-Project for the tenure 2025-26 aimed at interpreting ResNet-based CLIP models, through methods that involve inspecting their internal mechanisms. 

## Objectives
- Build intuition for Deep Convolutional Neural Networks process and represent visual information in image classification tasks
- Understand chosen behaviours in the shared  latent space between language and images in CLIP.
- Design and conduct controlled experiments to understand the internal mechanisms of ResNet-based CLIPs, including convolutional operators, feature maps, and learned representations
